Food Brands I Love

Enjoy Life

Enjoy Life Foods is recognized around the world as being the best source for tasty, allergy-friendly snacks. They have my stamp of approval!

Free of Top 14 Allergens, non-GMO, gluten free, and made with all natural ingredients

Made Good

Granola Bars are the perfect snack for when you are on the go! Not only do they contain a serving of vegetables in each bar, but they also past the taste test.

Free of Top 8 Allergens, non-GMO, gluten free, organic,and contain the nutrients in one full serving of vegetables from six different sources

Glutino

Glutino makes a variety of products focusing around gluten free. My personal favorites include their animal crackers and preztels. Perfect for kids!

Free of Gluten, peanuts, tree nuts, wheat, and milk

Bread Srsly

This bread is seriously good. It is only available to order online unless you live in SF, but trust me, it is worth the wait.

Free of Top 8 Allergens, Gluten, Potato, Tapioca, and Chickpeas

88 Acres

These granola bars are fantastic. Great for hikes and all other adventures. All ingredients come from single-source providers to reduce the risk of possible cross-contamination. Handcrafted in small batches, they taste better than homemade.

Free of Top 8 allergens, sesame, gluten, and made without use of latex gloves
