About the Blog

While Iâ€™m new to this blogging thing, I am a seasoned veteran when it comes to navigating a world full of allergens. While the "allergy free" journey seems especially difficult at times, it does get better! In fact, there is a large community of individuals, families, and peers (15 million in fact!) who are going through similar situations. With this blog, I am hoping to become a resource, a helping hand, and a watchful eye for the millions of people struggling to live a safe, stress free, and allergy free life. My goals for the blog are simple.
  • Educate my peers through allergy related articles
  • Connect with the 15 million Americans who are challenged every day by food allergies
  • Empower individuals to take back control of their life by providing resources/knowledge on topics specific to navigating the world while managing food allergies.
I believe God gives each of us gifts and talents to serve those around us and make this world a better place. In return, God gave us a wonderful place to live - full of sunshine, fresh air, and of course . . . allergens! Some of the gifts I have been blessed with include cooking, coaching, and being empathetic of others going through tough times. I hope you will find inspiration through my blog to discover your own gifts and talents, and use them to help make this world a better place.
